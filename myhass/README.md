## About

[MyHass.io][myhass-io] is a secure remote access service for Home Assistant.

This add-on establishes an outbound, encrypted connection to the
MyHass.io gateway, enabling safe external access without exposing
your home network. All communication is handled through a managed
tunnel, eliminating the need for open ports or complex firewall rules.

It is designed to provide a balance of convenience, security,
and reliability for remote Home Assistant access.

## Why MyHass.io?

The simplest way to start your smart home journey.

- End-to-End Encryption - Your data is encrypted from your instance to your device.
We can't see anything. Industry standard security.

- Zero Config - No port forwarding. No SSL hassles.

- Any Network - Works behind CGNAT and firewalls.

- Native Experience - Feels just like being home. Local network speeds, native app integration,
and full control at your fingertips.
